Adding Ion to Your Android Device's Home Screen

You can add Ion to your home screen for quick access using the following instructions:



  1. Open your preferred browser.
  2. Go to https://ion.tjhsst.edu.
  3. If prompted with an Install button, click it. Otherwise, continue to step 4:
  4. Click the menu button in the top right (the three dots) and tap Add to Home screen.
  5. Click Add on the pop-up.
  6. Ion should now be on your home screen!
